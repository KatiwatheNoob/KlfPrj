Permanent Residency

There are four categories of applications that can be made under Permanent Residency. These are provided under Section 37 of the Kenya Citizenship and Immigration Act, 2011. Choose applicable category of the applications below, to find out its requirements and apply.

Category A

Application for Permanent Residency by Ex-Citizens of Kenya

Category B

Application for Permanent Residency by holders of Work permits

Category C

Application for Permanent Residency by Children of Kenya Citizens

Category D

Application for Permanent Residency by Spouses of Kenya Citizens
