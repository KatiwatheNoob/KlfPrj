Dependent Pass

  1. Applicant must be:

a) Kenyan

b) Holder of a valid Entry permit, or

c) Exempted person under Immunities Act (Cap. 179) covered under section 4(3) (a) to (g).

‍

2. Note:

  • The Dependent Pass is exclusively designated for married couples.
  • It is prohibited to participate in trade or employment while holding a Dependent Pass.
  • Originalpassports of both applicant and dependent required during submission.

Requirements for Female/Child Dependent:

‍

1. Cover Letter:

  • Original cover letter from applicant, addressed to THE DIRECTOR OF IMMIGRATION SERVICES.

2. Application Form:

  • Form 28duly filled and signed.

3. Documentation:

  • Copy of passport, Kenyan ID, and Kenyan birth certificate (if Kenyan).
  • Copy of work permit (if non-Kenyan).
  • Copy of national passport of the dependent.
  • Two recent passport-size photos of the dependent.
  • Copy of birth certificates or marriage certificate (translated and certified).
  • Original Authorization Letter for Father’s Hand Limited (if using their services).

4. Fees:

  • KES 5,000 per dependent (cash).
  • Security bond ofKES 100,000 to be made with a bank or insurance company (to be done after approval).

Requirements for Male Dependent:

‍

1.Cover Letter:

  • Original cover letter from applicant, addressed to THE DIRECTOR OF IMMIGRATION SERVICES.

2. Application Form:

  • Form 28 duly filled and signed.

3. Documentation:

  • Copy of marriage certificate (translated and certified).
  • Copy of passport, Kenyan ID, or Kenyan birth certificate (if Kenyan).
  • Copy of work permit (if non-Kenyan).
  • Copy of national passport of the dependent.
  • Two recent passport-size photos of the dependent.
  • Proof of sufficient income from applicant.
  • Certified copy of sworn affidavit of incapacity.
  • Proof of any other condition leading to dependency.
  • Original Authorization Letter for Father’s Hand Limited (if using their services).

4. Fees:

  • KES 5,000 per dependent (cash).
  • Security bond ofKES 100,000to be made with a bank or insurance company (to be done after approval).

Once payments are submitted, the final pass will be processed and endorsed in the applicant’s passport after a few days.
